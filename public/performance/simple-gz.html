Always look at the source code.

The benchmarks game comparisons are often criticised and the criticism is often about the crowd-sourced programs.

Some say the programs are too optimised; they use foreign libraries too much; and splitters say the programs differ too much to be lumped together.

Here are original mandelbrot programs contributed in 2004 and 2005. Do even these simple programs differ too much for you? Are they just right — a Goldilocks comparison?
